These monthpickers use custom month adapter and formatter. The custom NgbMonthAdapter lets you use your own model i.e. the structure of ngModel. The custom NgbMonthParserFormatter lets you use your own formatter or parser i.e. the date format in input field.

Notice that there are two different services: the NgbMonthAdapter and the NgbMonthParserFormatter.
